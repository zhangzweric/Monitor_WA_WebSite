About Monitor WA

Philosophy

At Monitor WA quality products and services are our hallmark. It is this philosophy that has consolidated the highly respected Monitor reputation in our local market in Perth, and wherever our products are sold around the world. As is our motto, we strive to provide the best custom solutions to suit the many varied and very splendid needs of our clients.


Locally Owned and Operated

Monitor WA is proudly West Australian owned and operated. The company was established in 1993 and has grown steadily since then. An early success of the business, integrating the card production and printing services provided by the company, is the successful tender to produce the SmartRider travel cards in Western Australia, as reported by the West Australian newspaper here.

Since this time the company has been providing the local industry with state of the art systems in a wide variety of fields, including access control and print control, library gates, POS systems, smart cards and more. These days we enjoys a strong presence in our local area, providing systems and support to hundreds of local schools, hospitals, libraries and a host of other businesses and community groups. With technically experienced staff we continue to provide custom applications for each individual client to provide them with the best solution for their needs.
