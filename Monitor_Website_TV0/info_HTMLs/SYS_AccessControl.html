Access Control

Total Control is Monitor WA’s state of the art access control system. The system works by having a patron present an access card to an RFID reader that then communicates with the access server to determine if the patron is allowed entry to the area protected by the access point and whether they have sufficient funds (if the access point is setup for charging, such as may be used in a car park).

Total Control is feature-rich. A summary of the available features are presented below but the best way to see what the product is capable of is to arrange a demonstration from one of our trained sales staff – so call or email us today and learn why the people using Total Control love the product so much!

Features


Administration Software

  • Easy to use, attractive and powerful administration software provides access to all software configuration options.

Access Points

  • Integrated RFID reader, LEDs, buzzer and electronic lock.
  • POE power and data supply.
  • TCP/IP network connectivity.
  • Card reader poles and mounting brackets can be supplied.

Scheduled Events

  • Schedule access points to open or close at specified dates and times.

Kiosk

  • Deposit funds into your access account using notes, coins and credit cards via a Kiosk.

Automated SMS & Email Messages

  • Provide automatic updates to patrons with their current balance sent to their mobile phone, pager or email account weekly and/or whenever their balance runs low.

Integrated ID Card Orders

  • Monitor WA can print smart or PVC cards for you—with card orders being placed directly from the administration software. Details of new cards will be emailed to you and may be imported directly into Total Control for complete integration without manual data entry.

Highly Configurable

  • Sophisticated yet intuitive access permissions.
  • Optional entry charges at flat or metered standard and outside hours rates.

Import Contacts

  • Import patron details from a database, file or Outlook contacts.

Online Deposits

  • Deposit funds into your access account using a credit card or payment account via a web site.

Integrated ID Card Printing

  • Monitor WA can supply a card printer to suit every environment with accessories to match including colour, black & white, security and signature ribbons. Printed cards are active immediately once assigned to a patron.

Reports

  • Total Control provides a wide range of standard reports as well as customizable reports that may be viewed as tabular data or graphically as a pie, line or a bar chart.

Service & Support

  • Our friendly trained staff are on standby to provide phone, email and onsite support to all clients with support contracts.
