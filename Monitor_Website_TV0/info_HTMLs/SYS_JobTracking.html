Job Tracking

The job tracking module in Total Control was developed for internal use to track service department jobs at Monitor WA. Whilst this module can operate well as a standalone product, we envisage that most sites that require this module will use it specifically to track jobs relating to the core operation of Total Control.

Features


Administration Software

  • Easy to use, attractive and powerful administration software provides access to all software configuration options.

Job Tracking

  • Great care has been taken to allow users to create jobs using a minimal set of information, as little as a job summary, but may optionally include a lot of information including the contact person, assigned staff member, priority, state, project, job category and sub-category.
  • Multiple job notes may be associated with any job to provide a detailed historic record of issues reported and work undertaken.

Import Contacts

  • Import patron details from a database, file or Outlook contacts.

Reports

  • Total Control provides a wide range of standard reports as well as customizable reports that may be viewed as tabular data or graphically as a pie, line or a bar chart.

Service & Support

  • Our friendly trained staff are on standby to provide phone, email and onsite support to all clients with support contracts.
