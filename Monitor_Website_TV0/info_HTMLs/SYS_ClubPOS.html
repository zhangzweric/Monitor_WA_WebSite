Club POS

The Total Control Club POS module has been designed to provide an easy-to-use point of sale and stock tracking system that is suitable for sport and recreational clubs, and other institutions for which transactions are typically related to a patron account, card or event.

Features


Administration Software

  • Easy to use, attractive and powerful administration software provides access to all software configuration options.

Import Contacts

  • Import patron details from a database, file or Outlook contacts.

Cash, EFTPOS, Credit/Debit Card and Account Payments

  • The POS software accepts the standard cash, EFTPOS, Credit Card and Debit Card payments and also allows for payment from patron accounts stored in the system. Funds may be added to patron accounts from the administration software, a Kiosk or a web site.

Reward Points

  • Loyalty systems are extremely common these days and provide an excellent mechanism to encourage brand recognition and reward customer loyalty. All stock purchased through the POS system may optionally earn reward points for patrons, and in turn be purchased using accrued points. Reward points are not mandatory, and it is possible to set some or all stock items to reward no points, and/or to prevent them from being purchased with reward points.

Online Deposits

  • Deposit funds into your access account using a credit card or payment account via a web site.

Refunds

  • The POS software provides full or partial refunds of previous transactions, with refunded funds being paid out in cash or to an account.

Offline Transactions

  • In case of disconnection from the server, the POS software will prompt the operator to either wait for the server connection to resume or to continue in offline mode. Whilst there are some severe limitations to working in offline mode, it is often a better option than the alternative which is to have your POS terminals inactive until the server connection is reestablished. Whilst in offline mode the POS software will clearly indicate the offline state and continue trying to reconnect in the background. During this time, all sales must be rung up as cash purchases and payment may only be made using cash or EFTPOS (account payments are not available).

Stock Control

  • Monitor stock levels in real time and generate reports of recommended stock orders
  • Perform incremental or periodic stock takes
  • Track the profitability of stock over time

Shared Accounts

  • Shared accounts provide a common account pool that may be used by a group of patrons associated with the account. For example, members of a rugby team may have a shared account for their post-game celebrations that allows members of the team to make purchases using the shared account, and also deposit fund into that account if they wish.

Events

  • Events may be configured to occur on a single occasion or on a recurring basis. Each event may be associated with an account so that spending for the event comes out of that account. For instance, if a room were hired out for a party then the POS terminals in that room would be set to use the account for that event.

Kiosk

  • Deposit funds into your access account using notes, coins and credit cards via a Kiosk.

Reports

  • Total Control provides a wide range of standard reports as well as customizable reports that may be viewed as tabular data or graphically as a pie, line or a bar chart.

Integrated ID Card Printing

  • Monitor WA can supply a card printer to suit every environment with accessories to match including colour, black & white, security and signature ribbons. Printed cards are active immediately once assigned to a patron.
