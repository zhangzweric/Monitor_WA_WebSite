SmartRider Travel Cards

Monitor WA has produced SmartRider student concession cards for the WA public transport system since 2005. Our knowledge of the system and the prevalence of smart cards in Western Australian schools because of the early and widespread adoption of the SmartRider system has allowed Monitor WA and the schools to leverage the presence of the cards, at greatly reduced cost to the school, into diverse other systems including access control, attendance, POS, print management and more.

For more information about SmartRider cards, or to order a card online, please refer to our SmartRider portal.
