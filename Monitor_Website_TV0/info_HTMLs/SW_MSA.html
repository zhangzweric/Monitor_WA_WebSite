My Student Account

The My Student Account (MSA) system developed by IntilliSmart is the premiere school POS system in Australia. This solution offers schools a wide variety of options including:

  • POS software with customizable button layers and functionality.
  • Cashless purchases achieved using Smart Cards to avoid bullying, theft and lost funds. In WA the system is typically setup to work with the SmartRider concession cards already owned by most students.
  • Synchronization with existing student management systems.
  • A customizable per-student blacklist of prohibited purchases such as junk food or foods that contain allergens.
  • Online account top-up and ordering via a web site hosted by IntilliSmart using a credit card or other online payment method.
  • On-site account top-up via a Monitor WA Kiosk using cash or EFTPOS.
