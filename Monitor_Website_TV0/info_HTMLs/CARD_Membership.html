Choosing the Right Technology

Choosing the right technology for producing your cards is important! Smaller batches (fewer than 1000 cards) are normally produced on our in-house card printers and have an excellent quality for printed text, photos and most logos. For superior quality, including higher resolution printing but particularly if you require broad bands of a single color, then screen printing is the recommended if the order is of sufficient size; for larger orders screen printing is exceptionally cost-effective but for smaller orders the setup cost is prohibitive. A combination of screen printing and heat-transfer printing is also common, with the screen printing used to produce the card background at a very high quality and heat transfer being used to add personalized details such as the patron name and photo to the card. Our sales team can ease the burden of choice by recommending the best technology to suit your situation.
