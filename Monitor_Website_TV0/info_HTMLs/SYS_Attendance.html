Attendance

Total Control’s attendance tracking module is an extension of the Total Control Access Control system and works either as part of a standalone system or in conjunction with the access control. When used as a standalone system, patrons with RFID cards registered on the system can log their arrival and departure just by presenting their smart card to an access point. Used in conjunction with the access control module, the system allows for restricted use of the access points by patrons so that staff can only log their arrival and departure when they’re in the appropriate area. The administration software for the system provides a clear summary of staff arrival, departure and total hours per-day and per-week and allows for simple corrections whenever a patron forgets to log an arrival or departure.

Built on the powerful Total Control engine, the system allows for custom reports that may display data as a table, pie chart, line chart or bar chart. All reports may be saved to disk or printed, and tabular reports also provide exports into a wide variety of common formats including PDF, Excel and CSV.

Features


Administration Software

  • Easy to use, attractive and powerful administration software provides access to all software configuration options.

Attendance Tracking

  • Tracking is achieved by smartcards assigned to staff that may be presented to an access point to register an arrival or departure. Administrative staff may view daily, weekly and per-patron attendance at a glance, with colour coding to indicate short or long durations that require investigation — such as may occur if a staff member forgets to login or out — or delve deeper using the date and other filters provided. Users with appropriate permissions may add, edit and delete attendance records to correct errors and omissions to create an accurate attendance log.

Import Contacts

  • Import patron details from a database, file or Outlook contacts.

Leave

  • Various types of leave, including personal and sick leave, long service and maternity leave, may be tracked by the system and appear on the daily and weekly attendance summaries.
  • Having a permanent record of sick leave and other unscheduled leave allows administration staff monitor attendance trends.

Access Points

  • Integrated RFID reader, LEDs, buzzer and electronic lock.
  • POE power and data supply.
  • TCP/IP network connectivity.
  • Card reader poles and mounting brackets can be supplied.

Reports

  • Total Control provides a wide range of standard reports as well as customizable reports that may be viewed as tabular data or graphically as a pie, line or a bar chart.

Integrated ID Card Printing

  • Monitor WA can supply a card printer to suit every environment with accessories to match including colour, black & white, security and signature ribbons. Printed cards are active immediately once assigned to a patron.

Integrated ID Card Orders

  • Monitor WA can print smart or PVC cards for you—with card orders being placed directly from the administration software. Details of new cards will be emailed to you and may be imported directly into Total Control for complete integration without manual data entry.

Service & Support

  • Our friendly trained staff are on standby to provide phone, email and onsite support to all clients with support contracts.
