Card Printers

Monitor WA is the West Australian distributor for the Evolis range of card printers and printer accessories. We use these products in our card bureau and have no hesitation in recommending the products to our clients.

The most common card printers for commercial use are described briefly below. Most models displayed here allow an optional MiFare, 125 kHz, magnetic or other type of card encoder for producing smart cards.

Information about printer ribbons and accessories can be found on this web site. To view the entire product range, or for greater detail about a particular product, please refer to the Evolis web site.

Zenius

The Zenius is recommended for producing high quality, single sided cards singly or in short print runs.

  • Single-side printing module, edge-to-edge printing
  • Color-dye sublimation and monochrome thermal transfer
  • 300 dpi print head (11.8 dots/mm)
  • 16 MB of RAM
  • Color: from 120 to 150 cards/hr
    Monochrome: from 400 to 500 cards/hr
    		•

    Primacy

    The Primacy supports dual sided printing in addition to the features provided by the other models, and prints cards at a faster rate.

  • Single-side or dual-sided printing module, edge-to-edge printing
  • Color-dye sublimation and monochrome thermal transfer
  • 300 dpi print head (11.8 dots/mm)
  • 16 MB of RAM
  • Color (YMCKO): from 190 to 210 cards/hr
    Monochrome: from 600 to 850 cards/hr
    Dual side (YMCKO-K): 140 cards/hr
    		•

    Pebble and Dualys

    The Pebble extends the performance and specifications of the Zenius, boasting a card feeder with capacity for 100 cards, a slightly faster print rate and the ribbons are capable of printing 200 cards.

  • Color printing module
  • 300 dpi print head (11.8 dots/mm)
  • USB and Ethernet TCP-IP connections
  • 16 MB of RAM
  • Detachable feeder with a capacity of 100 cards (0.76mm - 30mil)
  • Pebble - for single-sided id badge printing in high-definition and four-color process
  • Pebble - output hopper with a capacity of 50 cards
  • Dualys - for dual-sided printing in high-definition and four-color process
  • Dualys - output hopper with a capacity of 100 cards
