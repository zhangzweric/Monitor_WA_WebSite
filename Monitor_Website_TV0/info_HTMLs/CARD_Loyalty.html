Loyalty Cards

Loyalty cards are a great way to build brand recognition and reward your customers for their continued custom. Similar to membership cards, loyalty cards also include a method to recognize the card electronically, such as a chip in a MiFare or other smart card, a magnetic strip or a barcode.

The ability to recognize a loyalty card electronically makes them suitable for use with a myriad of systems including access control, attendance, POS, print management and more.
